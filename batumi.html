BATUMI

Batumi is a beautiful city on the coast of the Black Sea in Georgia. It is known for its unique atmosphere, combining historical heritage, modern entertainment, and breathtaking seaside scenery.

One of the main attractions in Batumi is its promenade, which is one of the most popular places for strolling and relaxation. Here, you can enjoy the beauty of the sea while taking in the view from the giant Ferris wheel or visiting numerous restaurants and cafes.
Batumi is also a great place for entertainment and active leisure. Visitors can try their luck at the casinos, take a walk in the amusement park, or enjoy a yacht tour. The city is also renowned for its festivals, including the Batumi International Film Festival and the Festival of Aromas.

Furthermore, Batumi is known for its wonderful beaches. You can relax on the sandy shores, swim in the sea, or engage in water sports. The beach area offers a variety of services and entertainment for tourists.

Whether you are seeking history, culture, entertainment, or simply a peaceful seaside retreat, Batumi has a lot to offer. Its beauty and hospitality will make your stay unforgettable.
